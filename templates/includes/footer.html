Terrarium Supplies Ireland

Terrarium Supplies Ireland is an Irish owned company based in the West of Ireland. As terrarium enthusiasts, and lovers of all things nature and decor, we found it frustrating that buying our terrarium supplies from multiple stores online (and paying huge shipping costs too!). So, in November 2021, we decided to do something about it.
Our store was set up to allow nature lovers, interior decorators, and those of us who love to create beautiful things for our homes, a "one-stop-shop" for everything we need!
Through our site and our social media pages we hope to bring more people into the terrarium fold; by running a series of tutorials; what is a terrarium, how to build one and what supplies you might need. Sign up for our newsletter to get the most up to date info on our classes!
